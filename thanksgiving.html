Thanksgiving

1/23/26

Today, I am feeling thankful because I woke up in the morning. Then, I went on a run and called my mom. I am also thankful for the great food I was able to eat, and that I was not in despair. Even when I'm tired, I should not give up trying to be the best version of myself. For that, I am thankful.

1/26/26

The last few days I was so physically sick and in despair. I am thankful because I woke up today feeling a bit better. I also had more clarity, so I am thankful for that. I am thankful that I had a dream about my sister that in the middle of the night I felt love and remembered my family and what matters to me. I am thankful that I am able to lead an extraordinary Life with extraordinary people by my side. I ams so thankful that I was born into my family, for that is the gift that keeps me going through the toughest times of my life.

Lord, I pray that today you will fill me with joy that doesn't depreciate. Lord, I have been so depressed that I have ran out of tears. Lord, I know that my faith and righteous actions have dwindled, but I believe that you will make me better. I need to believe that because I see that otherwise there is no hope. It is not about escaping here and being good in NY, I want to be blessed by You in every single step that I take.

Lord, I pray this but am so short of faith that I don't believe, even as I pray. Would you give me the faith to have hope and believe? Lord, would you heal me physically and mentally and most importantly, spiritually Lord?

Lord, would you show me what you see in me? I don't see a lick of goodness in me. Lord, I know that all my goodness is from You, but when I see myself without You or your goodness, I am so worthless and weak. Could you give me the faith to know that even if I am this way, You will pull me through?
